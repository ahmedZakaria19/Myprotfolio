About Me

My journey into the world of tech started back in high school. I remember looking at websites and apps, wondering: 'How do they turn lines of code into these amazing interfaces?' That curiosity was the spark. Now, as a student at the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence, that 'how' has become my daily reality. I decided to specialize in Frontend and Mobile Development because I love the thrill of building things people can see, touch, and interact with. There’s a special kind of joy in seeing an idea transform from a blank screen into a functional application. I don’t just write code; I craft digital experiences with a passion for clean design and smooth performance.
